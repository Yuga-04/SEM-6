**Answer:** Sensor.

**Answer:** Analog sensor.

**Answer:** Environmental / physical conditions. 

### 17. __________ are the “Things” in the Internet of Things.

**Answer:** Sensors and actuators.

**Answer:** Communication technologies.

**Answer:** To sense, process, store, and communicate data.

**Answer:** IEEE 802.15.4.

**Answer:** A device that converts control signals into physical action or mechanical motion.

**Answer:** Reliability.

**Answer:** Wireless communication technology.

**Answer:** The amount of data transmitted per unit time.
