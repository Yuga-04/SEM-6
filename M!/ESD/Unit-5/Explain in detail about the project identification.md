# Project Identification

Project identification is the first and most important step in starting a new business venture. It involves discovering and selecting suitable project opportunities for investment and development. A project is an envisioned work plan devised by an entrepreneur or top management to achieve specific objectives within a specified period of time. 

## Meaning of Project Identification

Project identification refers to the process of finding feasible and promising project ideas. It involves the collection, compilation, and analysis of economic and business information to identify investment opportunities.

According to the PDF:

* Project identification is the first step of a new venture.
* It is crucial for entrepreneurs to identify suitable projects.
* It includes the analysis of opportunities for investment and development. 

An entrepreneur has many choices regarding:

* Product or service
* Market
* Technology
* Equipment
* Scale of production
* Location
* Incentives
* Time phasing

The process of identifying a project is influenced by:

* Government policies
* Infrastructure development

# Types of Opportunities in Project Identification

According to Drucker, project opportunities are classified into three types:

## 1. Additive Opportunity

These opportunities help better utilize existing resources without changing the basic nature of the business.

### Features:

* Minimum disturbance to existing operations
* Least amount of risk
* Improves efficiency and productivity

### Example:

Increasing production capacity using existing machinery.

---

## 2. Complementary Opportunity

These opportunities involve introducing new ideas that complement the existing business.

### Features:

* Moderate changes in business structure
* Considerable amount of risk
* Expands existing activities

### Example:

A bakery introducing a coffee shop alongside its bakery products.

---

## 3. Breakthrough Opportunity

These opportunities involve major and fundamental changes in the structure and character of the business.

### Features:

* High innovation
* High risk
* Major transformation in business operations

### Example:

A traditional retail store shifting completely to an online e-commerce model.

# Steps Involved in Project Identification

The important steps involved are:

1. Identification of business opportunities
2. Market survey and demand analysis
3. Study of government policies and incentives
4. Analysis of technical and financial feasibility
5. Selection of suitable technology and location
