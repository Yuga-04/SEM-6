## What are the Main Objectives and Functions of DIC and SIDO? (15 Marks)

Small-scale industries require guidance, financial assistance, and technical support for their growth. In India, organizations such as **District Industries Centre (DIC)** and **Small Industries Development Organisation (SIDO)** were established to promote and support small-scale industries.

---

# 1. District Industries Centre (DIC)

The **District Industries Centre (DIC)** is a government institution established to promote and develop small-scale industries at the **district level**.

## Objectives of DIC

1. To promote the development of **small-scale industries in rural and semi-urban areas**.
2. To provide **guidance and support to entrepreneurs** for starting new industries.
3. To encourage **self-employment opportunities**.
4. To implement **government policies and schemes** for industrial development.
5. To help entrepreneurs utilize **local resources effectively**.

## Functions of DIC

1. Acts as a **single window agency** for the promotion and development of small-scale industries at the district level.
2. Conducts **industrial potential and techno-economic surveys**.
3. Implements **schemes of central and state governments** related to small industries.
4. Provides **guidance to entrepreneurs on all aspects of their projects**.
5. Helps in **project appraisal and preparation of project reports**.
6. Provides **marketing assistance** and other support services to entrepreneurs.

# 2. Small Industries Development Organisation (SIDO)

The **Small Industries Development Organisation (SIDO)** was established by the Government of India to promote and develop small-scale industries throughout the country.

## Objectives of SIDO

1. To promote **growth and development of small-scale industries**.
2. To coordinate **government policies and programs** related to small industries.
3. To provide **technical and managerial support** to entrepreneurs.
4. To improve **productivity and competitiveness** of small industries.

## Functions of SIDO

1. **Coordinate policies and programs** of the government related to industrial development.
2. Promote **industrial development in the small-scale sector**.
3. Provide **extension services such as technical consultancy and training** to entrepreneurs.
4. Assist small industries in improving **technology and management practices**.

# Conclusion

Organizations like **DIC and SIDO** play an important role in promoting small-scale industries in India. They provide **technical guidance, training, financial assistance, marketing support, and policy coordination** to help entrepreneurs establish and expand their enterprises.
