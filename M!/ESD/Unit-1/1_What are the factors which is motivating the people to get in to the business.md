## What are the Factors which Motivate People to Get into Business? (15 Marks)

Entrepreneurship is the process of starting and managing a business venture. Many individuals are motivated to enter business due to several personal, economic, and social factors. These motivating factors encourage people to become entrepreneurs and start their own enterprises. According to the PDF, various factors influence individuals to choose entrepreneurship as a career. 

---

# 1. Need for Independence

Many people start their own business because they want to be **independent and self-reliant**. Entrepreneurship allows individuals to be their own boss and make decisions freely.

---

# 2. Desire for Financial Rewards

One of the main motivations for entering business is the **opportunity to earn higher income and financial rewards**. Successful entrepreneurs can earn more compared to salaried employees.

---

# 3. Achievement Orientation

Some individuals have a strong **desire to achieve success and accomplish challenging goals**. Entrepreneurship provides them with opportunities to prove their abilities.

---

# 4. Implementation of Ideas

Entrepreneurship allows people to **implement their innovative ideas and creativity** by converting them into successful business ventures.

---

# 5. Opportunity to Handle All Aspects of Business

Entrepreneurs get the chance to **deal with various aspects of business such as production, marketing, finance, and management**, which attracts many individuals.

---

# 6. Job Insecurity

Lack of job security in salaried employment encourages people to **start their own businesses** to ensure a stable source of income.

---

# 7. Family Business Background

Individuals belonging to **business families** are often motivated to continue or expand their family business.

---

# 8. Risk-Taking Ability

Some people enjoy challenges and are willing to **take calculated risks**, which motivates them to start and run businesses.

---

# 9. Government Economic Policies

Supportive **government policies, incentives, and financial assistance** encourage individuals to start new enterprises.

---

# 10. Vision to Create a Lasting Enterprise

Many entrepreneurs have a vision to **build a long-lasting organization** that contributes to society and economic development.

---

# Conclusion

Several factors motivate individuals to enter business, including **independence, financial rewards, achievement motivation, innovative ideas, job insecurity, family background, and government support**. These motivating factors encourage people to become entrepreneurs and contribute to economic growth and employment generation.
