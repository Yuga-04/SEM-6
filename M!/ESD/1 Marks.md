# Unit – 1

1. **What do we call the act of starting and running a new business?** <br>
   **Answer:** Entrepreneurship. 

2. **What is the introduction of a new idea or method in business called?** <br>
   **Answer:** Innovation. 

3. **What is the term for uncertainty undertaken in business?** <br>
   **Answer:** Risk. 

4. **What is the financial gain from a business known as?** <br>
   **Answer:** Profit.

5. **What is the term for increasing the size of a business?** <br>
   **Answer:** Expansion.

6. **What is the freedom to make business decisions called?** <br>
   **Answer:** Independence (or Autonomy).

7. **What is strong self-belief referred to as?** <br>
   **Answer:** Self-confidence.

8. **What do we call the capacity to generate innovative and original ideas?** <br>
   **Answer:** Creativity. 

9. **What is the ability to guide and motivate others called?** <br>
   **Answer:** Leadership. 

10. **What is a strong drive to achieve success called?** <br>
    **Answer:** Motivation. 

11. **What is the ability to adjust to changing situations called?** <br>
    **Answer:** Adaptability. 

12. **What do we call a clear and long-term business direction?** <br>
    **Answer:** Vision.

13. **What is the activity of promoting and selling products known as?** <br>
    **Answer:** Marketing.

14. **What is the process of setting goals and outlining actions called?** <br>
    **Answer:** Planning.

15. **What do we call the ability to settle terms through discussion?** <br>
    **Answer:** Negotiation.

# Unit – 2

1. **Which environment includes banks and financial institutions?** <br>
   **Answer:** Economic environment. 

2. **Government tax policies are part of _____________ environment.** <br>
   **Answer:** Political / Legal environment. 

3. **Changes in demand and supply belong to ____________ environment.** <br>
   **Answer:** Economic environment. 

4. **Who provides primary support to an entrepreneur at the initial stage?** <br>
   **Answer:** Family. 

5. **Moral support from society builds _________.** <br>
   **Answer:** Confidence.

6. **Values and ethics in business are shaped by _________.** <br>
   **Answer:** Society (Socio-cultural environment). 

7. **What does EDP stand for?** <br>
   **Answer:** Entrepreneurship Development Programme.

8. **Training programs help improve ________.** <br>
   **Answer:** Entrepreneurial skills.

9. **Financial assistance to entrepreneurs is called _______.** <br>
   **Answer:** Financial support. 

10. **Institutions providing entrepreneur training are called ________.** <br>
    **Answer:** Entrepreneurship Development Institutions.

11. **Government financial support for small businesses is called?** <br>
    **Answer:** Subsidy.

12. **Industrial development in backward areas is encouraged through?** <br>
    **Answer:** Incentives.

13. **Tax reduction offered by government is known as?** <br>
    **Answer:** Tax concession.

14. **Policies that promote small-scale industries are called?** <br>
    **Answer:** Industrial policies.

15. **What does MSME stand for?** <br>
    **Answer:** Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises.

# Unit – 3

1. **_____ is a source of business idea.** <br>
   **Answer:** Customers. 

2. **What does PFS stand for?** <br>
   **Answer:** Pre-Feasibility Study. 

3. **________ is conducted for the purpose of determining whether the idea is worth pursuing.** <br>
   **Answer:** Pre-feasibility study. 

4. **________ criteria determines market demand and the price at which demanded.** <br>
   **Answer:** Market criteria. 

5. **________ criteria determine cash flow projection.** <br>
   **Answer:** Financial criteria. 

6. **_________ criteria determines human resource required and their skills.** <br>
   **Answer:** Organizational criteria. 

7. **________ of a business refers to legal control over a business.** <br>
   **Answer:** Ownership. 

8. **_______ is the process of evaluating investments to get best returns.** <br>
   **Answer:** Capital budgeting.

9. **The rate of return from the project should be ______ than the cost of financing that project.** <br>
   **Answer:** Higher.

10. **______ period determines the length of time in which an investment pays back its original cost.** <br>
    **Answer:** Payback period.

11. **What does ARR stand for?** <br>
    **Answer:** Accounting Rate of Return.

12. **What does NPV stand for?** <br>
    **Answer:** Net Present Value.

13. **_______ gives a bird’s eye view of the proposed project.** <br>
    **Answer:** Project profile.

14. **AirBnB is an example of _______ type of market served.** <br>
    **Answer:** Consumer market.

15. **Electronic sub-assemblies is an example of ________ type of market served.** <br>
    **Answer:** Industrial market.
